Welcome to Top Secret

Here you can encode or decode a message to have a secret conversation with your friends.

How does it work?

This site uses the Caesar Cipher technique to encode your message. This method replaces each letter with a different one a fixed number of places down the alphabet.

You can enter the message and set an offset number to see what the coded message will be.
